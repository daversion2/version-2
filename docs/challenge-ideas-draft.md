# Challenge Library Ideas Draft

> **Key:** Challenges marked with `[V]` have a natural variable parameter in the title.
> **Beginner** = Difficulty 1-2 | **Advanced** = Difficulty 3-5
> **Start** = Do the thing | **Resist** = Abstain from the thing

---
