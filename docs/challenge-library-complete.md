# Challenge Library — Complete

> **Format:** One block per challenge with all fields required for Firestore seeding.
> **Tone:** Blunt, direct, no-nonsense. Descriptions tell you what to do. Notes explain why it matters.
>
> **Field Reference:**
> - **Category** — Physical, Social, Mind
> - **Difficulty** — 1 (Easy) to 5 (Extreme)
> - **Action Type** — `complete` (Start: do the thing) or `resist` (Stop: abstain from the thing)
> - **Beginner Friendly** — `true` (Difficulty 1-2) or `false` (Difficulty 3-5)
> - **Barrier Type** — comfort-zone, delayed-gratification, discipline, ego, energy-drainer
> - **Time Category** — quick-win (1-15 min), ritual (16-45 min), deep-work (46-180 min), all-day (181+ min)
> - **`[V]`** — Challenge has a natural variable parameter in the title

### 1. Cold Water Face Splash

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Category | Physical |
| Difficulty | 1 |
| Action Type | complete |
| Beginner Friendly | true |
| Barrier Type | comfort-zone |
| Time Category | quick-win |
| Time (min) | 5 |
| Description | Splash cold water on your face for 30 seconds. No easing in, no lukewarm — full cold, the entire time. |
| Success Criteria | Face splashed with cold water for 30 continuous seconds. |
| Why | The lowest-barrier entry point to choosing discomfort on purpose. |
| Neuroscience | Cold water on the face triggers the mammalian dive reflex, activating the vagus nerve and shifting your nervous system from sympathetic (fight-or-flight) to parasympathetic (calm). Deliberately choosing this discomfort trains your prefrontal cortex to override your amygdala's avoidance signals. |
| Psych Benefit | Builds confidence that you can handle discomfort and act despite resistance. |
| What You'll Learn | That the anticipation of discomfort is almost always worse than the discomfort itself. |
| Common Resistance | ["It's too cold", "This seems pointless", "I'll do it tomorrow"] |
| Real World Examples | ["At the sink after brushing your teeth", "In the shower before you turn the warm water on", "During a midday reset when you need to refocus"] |
| Variations | Easier: 15 seconds instead of 30 / Harder: 60 seconds / Advanced: Full cold water over your head |
