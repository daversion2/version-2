The thoughts that tend to surface when you're still? They don't go away when you stop focusing on them — they follow you into your decisions, your habits, and your behavior. Over time, the patterns you notice here can be altered to align with your goals.

That's why we use a redirect mantra. When a negative or unhelpful thought surfaces, your mantra is what you reach for. Your brain can only hold one thought at a time — give it something better to hold onto.

Next time you catch yourself in a negative thought pattern, bring this mantra to the forefront of your mind and put it on repeat to re-focus.
